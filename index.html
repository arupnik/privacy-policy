Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Introduction

Rupnik Home ("the App") is a personal household management application. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties. All data entered into the App is stored exclusively on a private, self-hosted server controlled by the developer and is not accessible to any external parties.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for push notifications. FCM may collect device tokens required for delivering notifications. No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

Advertising

The App does not display advertisements and does not use advertising identifiers.

Data Security

All communication between the App and the server is encrypted using HTTPS. Access to the App requires authentication via username and password.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: admin@rupnik.io
